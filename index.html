Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4th November 2025

SparkVibe (“we”, “our”, or “us”) built the mobile application “QR Scanner : Barcode Generator” (“the App”) as a utility tool for scanning, generating, and sharing QR codes and barcodes.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App. We (the developer) do not collect, store, or share any personal data. Limited data is shared directly with trusted third parties for specific features.

1. Information We Collect

The developer collects no data. All QR/barcode processing happens locally on your device.

Third-party data sharing:
• Scanned URLs → Google Safe Browsing (malware/phishing check)
• Ads → Google AdMob (non-personalized, G-rated ads)

2. Permissions We Use and Their Purpose

Camera

Used only for live scanning. Images are processed locally and never uploaded.

Photos and Media (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_MEDIA_VIDEO)

Used only when you save or load generated QR/Barcode codes from your gallery. The app never uploads, shares, or analyzes your personal photos or videos. All access remains strictly local to your device.

Storage (Read/Write)

To save/load generated QR/Barcode images. Files stay on your device.

Internet & Network Access

Required for:

  • Google Safe Browsing API: Sends scanned URLs to check for threats.
  • Google AdMob: Loads and displays non-personalized ads.

All connections use encrypted HTTPS.

Location

Optional for creating location or Wi-Fi QR codes. Used only when you request it.

3. Data Shared with Third Parties

Google Safe Browsing API

When you scan a URL, only the link is sent to Google for safety verification. No personal information is attached.

Google Privacy Policy

Google AdMob

Displays non-personalized, child-appropriate (G-rated) ads. AdMob may collect limited technical data such as:

  • Device ID (for ad delivery frequency control)
  • Approximate location (for regional compliance)
  • App usage & crash data
This data is processed by Google in accordance with its Privacy Policy.

AdMob Privacy Info

Opt out: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID or opt out of personalization.

4. Local Data Storage

Scan history & generated images are stored via Hive database — on your device only.

Delete anytime via Android Settings → Apps → Clear data.

5. Data Security

  • No servers = no developer access
  • HTTPS for all network calls
  • Temporary (ephemeral) processing for Safe Browsing checks

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is designed to be suitable for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Ads served through Google AdMob are configured to show only non-personalized, child-appropriate (G-rated) content. No interest-based or behavioral ad targeting is used.

Parents and guardians who believe that their child has provided us with personal information may contact us at hraza2690@gmail.com to request deletion.
