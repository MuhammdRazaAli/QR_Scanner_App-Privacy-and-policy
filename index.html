Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

SparkVibe (“we”, “our”, or “us”) built the mobile application “Barcode Scanner : QR Generator” (“the App”) as a utility tool for scanning, generating, and sharing QR codes and barcodes.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not automatically collect, transmit, or share personal or sensitive data.

All information processed by the App remains stored locally on your device unless you explicitly choose to share or use it (e.g., by generating or scanning a QR code).

2. Permissions We Use and Their Purpose

Camera

Used solely to scan barcodes and QR codes.

Images captured are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded or stored on any external server.

Storage (Read/Write)

Used to:

  • Save generated QR or barcode images to your device’s gallery
  • Retrieve saved images for re-use or sharing

All files remain stored locally on your device, and we do not access or transmit them externally.

Internet & Network Access

Used only for:

  • Connecting securely to Google Safe Browsing API or similar trusted APIs to check whether a scanned link may be malicious or unsafe.

No analytics, advertising, or user-tracking data are collected.

All network communication is encrypted via HTTPS.

Location (Fine and Coarse)

Used in two cases:

  1. When a user creates a location-based QR code (e.g., map link or event QR), the app may request location access to insert your current coordinates.
  2. Some Android functions (such as Wi-Fi scanning) require location permission to detect nearby networks or encode Wi-Fi details in a QR code.

The App does not continuously track, record, or share your location. Location information is used only at your request and remains on your device.

Contacts, Call, and SMS

These permissions appear because the App allows you to generate QR codes containing contact information, phone numbers, or SMS messages.

The App does not automatically access your contacts or messages — it only uses data you manually input.

3. Data Stored Locally

The App may store:

  • Scan history
  • Generated QR/barcode images
  • Locally created QR content (text, links, events, etc.)

All such data is stored using Hive, a secure local database.

You have full control and can delete this data at any time by clearing the app data or removing the files manually.

4. Use of Third-Party Services

The App uses Google Safe Browsing API to verify whether scanned URLs are safe.

When you scan a link, only that link may be transmitted to Google for security verification — no personal information or identifying data are shared.

For more details, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Security

We value your privacy:

  • No data is uploaded to or stored on our servers.
  • All user information remains local to your device.
  • Any network communication (for Safe Browsing checks) is protected via encrypted HTTPS.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Because the App operates mostly offline and requires manual user input, it is safe for general use.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally.

When updates are made, the revised date will appear at the top of this document.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
